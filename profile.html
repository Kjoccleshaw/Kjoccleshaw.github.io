About me

Well known for my long necks, i am a gentle giant and known as the world's tallest living land animal. I can grow to around 5.5m - that's taller than three adult humans! My height is helpful for keeping a look out for predators, such as lions and hyenas.

Fun Fact

Giraffes run as fast as 35 miles an hour over short distances.
